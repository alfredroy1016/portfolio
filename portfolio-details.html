Cybersecurity is a crucial and dynamic field dedicated to protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. This involves implementing robust security measures, monitoring for suspicious activities, and responding to incidents swiftly to mitigate damage. The domain encompasses various specializations, including network security, application security, information security, and operational security, each focusing on different aspects of safeguarding the digital world.
